## Understanding the BioGlyph Terminology
- **Multispecific Antibody (MsAb) Format** – A collection of two or more Building Blocks
- **Building Block (BB)** - is a functional group with an intended biology
- **Domain** - it has an independent folded structure but may not have functional activity on its own.
- **Annotation** – Label within a amino acid sequence
- **Sequence** – Amino acid sequence
